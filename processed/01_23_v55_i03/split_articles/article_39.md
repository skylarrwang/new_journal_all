**Title: Gilgamesh to Enkidu**
**Author: Edie Lipsey**
**Page number(s): 54**

We are becoming human together
Washing dishes in the morning
Pulling spoons from the disposal
You move in your sleep so much it startles me
Temple to Temple
We are slow to rise 
Wildly,
You say,
It takes so long to become a person
